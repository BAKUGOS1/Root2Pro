# Root2Pro Content Style Guide

## Voice

Write like a senior friend explaining a topic clearly.

## Lesson structure

1. What you will learn
2. Why it matters
3. Simple explanation
4. Example
5. Practice
6. Common mistake
7. Recall
8. Next step

## Avoid

- Robotic definitions
- Long theory without examples
- Copy-pasted documentation
- Too much jargon too early
